There are three basic types of transformation rules available:

  * Formatters
  * Translators
  * Generators


## Generators ##
  * Markov (n-gram) Chains

## Translators ##
  * Leet
  * Pig Latin
  * Shuffle
  * Disemconsonant
  * Disemvowell
  * Random Caps
  * Reverse
  * Shouty Caps
  * Vowell to Punctuation
  * Homphonerizer
  * Punctuize Whitespace

## Formatters ##
  * Short Lines
  * Initial Spaces
  * Free Verse
  * Heijinian Aid to Memory
  * XraysMonaLisa-style
